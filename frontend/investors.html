01 A category nobody is yet building

For the last fifteen years the identity industry sold convenience as a category. Single-sign-on, password reset, social login. Sprinkle MFA, ship more SAML, raise an IPO. That category is now obsolete. The buyer who matters in 2027 to 2033 is not a SaaS director. It is a CIO of a regulated bank, a defence prime, a sovereign cloud, a ministry, a hospital network. Their procurement team is reading the NSA CNSA 2.0 directive and the OMB M-23-02 cryptographic-inventory mandate. They cannot buy a product whose roadmap says “post-quantum coming.”

MILNET is built for that buyer. Hybrid post-quantum is the default, not a feature flag. Token issuance is threshold-signed across five hosts. Nineteen safety invariants are mechanically checked in TLA+. Every audit entry is BFT-signed and Merkle-anchored. This is the first identity product that a regulator, an auditor, and a quantum threat-modeller can all sign off on without asking for a roadmap commitment.

“A 2027 procurement officer who buys a non-PQ identity vendor will face their own audit committee in 2030.”

03 Four pillars in code · not roadmap, not vendor demo
Pillar 1

Post-Quantum Native

Hybrid X25519 + ML-KEM-1024 in the session ratchet. ML-DSA-87 on every issued token, every audit entry, every transparency-log leaf. SLH-DSA, LMS, XMSS reserves for code signing. FIPS 140-3 startup KATs gate every primitive at boot.

Pillar 2

Threshold Everywhere

FROST-Ristretto255 3-of-5 threshold token signing with persistent fsync’d nonce log. 2-of-3 threshold OPAQUE: no single server can recover the password OPRF. Pedersen DKG + 3-of-5 quorum to issue any internal certificate.

Pillar 3

Formally Verified

1,081-line TLA+ specification with 19 mechanically-checked safety invariants plus 3 liveness properties. Models the auth state machine, FROST DKG, OPAQUE, ratchet, Bell-LaPadula, key rotation, multi-tenancy, CAE re-evaluation. Constant-time TLA+ + dudect Welch t-test.

Pillar 4

Audit + Transparency

11-node Byzantine Fault Tolerant audit cluster, ML-DSA-87 signed and hash-chained. Out-of-process witness signer over UDS. Key Transparency log with 2-of-5 ML-DSA-87 consensus and CSHAKE256 leaves. Independent auditor service.
